Ars Automata is a collection of experimental prose derived from cellular automata. Specifically, it explores the British mathmetician John Horton Conway's Game of Life as a means of poetry, creating didactic metaphors evolved from a semantic view of the computational, the mathematical, and the autonomous.
 Ars Automata was conceived, designed, developed, and authored by Aldon Chen in February of 2021, who continues to develop the project. A special thanks to Stephen Hendee, Daniel Shiffman, and the Pangram Pangram Foundry.
